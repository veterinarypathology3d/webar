Veterinary Pathology 3D

Veterinary Pathology experience with WebAR (first webAR veterinary pathology app in the world). Veterinary Pathology 3D is a unique webAR app (Web-Augmented Reality) that enables students to interactively model pathological changes on 3D models of animal organs (only kidney for now). This app enables veterinary students to improve their skills in recognition of morphological changes and knowledge in pathology.

This web application was created as a result of the following original article.

"Building a Web-Augmented Reality application for demonstration of kidney pathology for veterinary education" by
 H.T. Atmaca and O.S. Terzi in "Polish Journal of Veterinary Sciences | 2021 | vol. 24 | No 3 | 345-350 | DOI: 10.24425/pjvs.2021.137671

How to use this web app.

1

open website with pc, mac, tablet or mobile web browser

safari, firefox, chrome, edge etc...
2

choose the 3D kidney model, then free control of 3D pathology model 

3

if you connect with AR supported android* or ios** mobile device, 
press switch AR (augmented reality) button (automatic displayed) on screen 

*A wide variety of qualified Android phones running Android 7.0 (Nougat) and later. ARCore via Google Play Services for AR installed devices.

**Iphone 6s and above, iPad Pro (all models) iPad Air (4th generation) iPad Air (3rd generation) iPad (5th generation or later) iPad mini (5th generation).
