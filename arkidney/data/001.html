Normal kidney

This is a cross section of normal kidney.

Gross inspection should include, size, shape, color, and consistency.

 A kidney size is generally about 3 vertebrae long.

The kidneys are normally bean- or horseshoe-shaped, 

but the bovine kidney is lobated externally. 

The normal cortex-to-medulla ratio in this plane is about 1 : 2 or 1 : 3. 

The cortex normally rate as 80% of the renal mass, 

a fact that is better understanding in a coronal section , than in a sagittal section.
